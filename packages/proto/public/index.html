Recipes

Ingredients

  • Carrot
  • Beef
  • Red Wine
  • Beef Stock
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Saute onions and carrots.
  2. Remove from pan, deglaze with red wine and add beef to pan.
  3. When brown, add stock and vegetables.
  4. After cooking, add heavy cream, salt, and pepper.
